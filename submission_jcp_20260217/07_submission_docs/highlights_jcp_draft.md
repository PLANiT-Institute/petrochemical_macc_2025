# JCP Highlights (Evidence-Locked Draft)

- Facility-level evidence (243 facilities, 2025-2050) shows petrochemical decarbonization is constrained by infrastructure scale-up, not only cost ranking.
- The high-ambition pathway requires 7.273 BUSD/yr in 2030 for 1.902 MtCO2/yr abatement and reaches 25.043 BUSD/yr with 51.505 MtCO2/yr abatement in 2050.
- Electricity-centered pathways require about 146.034 TWh/yr added electricity demand in 2050.
- Hydrogen-centered pathways require about 4.546 Mt/yr hydrogen and 32.380 TWh/yr added electricity demand in 2050.
- Policy relevance: credible transition planning requires synchronized grid and hydrogen infrastructure programs.
