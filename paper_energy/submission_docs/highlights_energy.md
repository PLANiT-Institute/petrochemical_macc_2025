# Highlights — Energy

- Electrification pathways need 146 TWh/yr added electricity by 2050
- Hydrogen pathways require 4.5 Mt/yr H2 plus 32 TWh/yr electricity
- Energy costs are 93–98% of total transition expenditures
- Energy price assumptions drive ~500 trillion KRW cost difference
- Grid constraints raise 2050 costs by 17 BUSD/yr (68% premium)
